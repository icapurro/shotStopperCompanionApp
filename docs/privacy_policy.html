Privacy Policy

ShotStopper is developed as a companion app for a hardware device. This app is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform users about our policies regarding data collection and usage.

Data Collection and Use

ShotStopper does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The app operates locally on your device and solely communicates with the paired hardware device via Bluetooth.

However, to improve the app's performance and stability, we may collect anonymized usage analytics and crash reports. This information is used solely for debugging and enhancing the user experience.

Crash Logs and Analytics

ShotStopper may collect and transmit non-personal data such as:

  • App usage statistics (e.g., feature interactions)
  • Crash logs, including error reports, device model, operating system version, and app version

This data is collected using third-party services, such as Google Firebase or similar analytics tools, to help diagnose issues and improve the app. No personally identifiable information is included in these reports.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to process crash logs and usage analytics. These services have their own privacy policies regarding data collection and processing.

Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure that any data collected is secure and used only for improving the app. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

ShotStopper does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. Since no personal information is collected, there is no risk of exposure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
