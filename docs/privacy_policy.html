Privacy Policy

ShotStopper is a companion app for the ShotStopper Arduino board, an innovative brew-by-weight add-on for espresso machines. This app is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page informs users about our policies regarding data collection, usage, and permissions.

Data Collection and Use

ShotStopper does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

However, the app requires certain permissions to function properly, including access to Bluetooth and Location Services for connecting to the ShotStopper device.

Location Data

ShotStopper requests access to Location Services because Android requires it for scanning and connecting to Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) devices.

  • Why is Location Required? Android requires Location Services to be enabled for BLE scanning, even though ShotStopper does not track or store your location.
  • Does ShotStopper Collect or Share Location Data? No. The app does not store, transmit, or use your location for any purpose other than enabling BLE communication.

Crash Logs and Analytics

To improve app performance, ShotStopper may collect anonymized usage data and crash reports, including:

  • App usage statistics (e.g., feature interactions)
  • Crash logs (device model, OS version, error reports)

This data is processed using third-party services such as Firebase or similar tools and does not include personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for crash reporting and analytics. These services have their own privacy policies regarding data collection and processing.

Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure that any collected data remains secure and is used solely to improve the app experience. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

ShotStopper does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
